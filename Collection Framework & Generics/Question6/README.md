6. You are tasked with managing a list of Product objects for an e-commerce application.
Define a Product class with properties name, price and rating (double). Create a
LinkedList<Product> to store 5 products, and add products to the list.
a. Iterate over the list and display all product names, their prices, and rating.
b. Find the product with the highest price (consider each product has a unique price) and
display the product details.
c. Sort the product based on their rating in descending order.
d. Remove all the products with rating less than 2.5.
